SEDAN

A long-running and popular body style among car buyers around the world, the sedan adapts nicely to almost any automotive lifestyle. Whether you are a single person or part of a family of five, a sedan provides several useful features such as four doors and a separate trunk storage area. Sedans are unofficially classified under the category family sedan, sports sedan or luxury sedan. Priced within reach of most new car customers, family sedans sell interior space and practicality as high points. If looking for a family sedan, choices include the Honda Accord, Nissan Altima, Ford Fusion and Toyota Camry. Once considered dull to some drivers, growing standard features lists for family sedans will often include convenience items such high-tech stereo systems and Bluetooth connectivity.
